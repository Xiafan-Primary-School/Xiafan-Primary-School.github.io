About Us

Dongyang Jiangbei Xiafan Primary School was founded in 1921. In 2021, we celebrated our centennial. We have always been committed to providing quality education for all school-age childrens, so that children can grow up healthy and happy, and learn knowledge in a happy atmosphere,which is our lifelong pursuit.

Our Faculty

We have first-class teachers in the city and even in the province. Our teachers have won many awards in municipal and provincial competitions, and have also been highly praised in the industry. I have many senior primary school teachers, covering many subjects such as Chinese, mathematics, English, and (natural) science..

Our Courses

Our curriculum is rich, covers a variety of directions, covers a wide range of knowledge, covers a wide range of subjects, and enables children to develop in all aspects. In addition to the main curriculum, our school is also actively exploring new courses and starting a variety of interest clubs, and children can choose according to their own interests.
